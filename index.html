Ultimate Road Runner

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Overview

Ultimate Road Runner ("we", "our", or "the game") is a free mobile arcade game developed by Rock City, in which a chicken tries to cross a busy road. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you play the game.

The game itself collects no personal data. We do not have login systems, user accounts, databases, or any mechanism to identify, track, or store information about individual players. Your score, progress, and gameplay exist only on your device.

Advertising — What Is Collected and Why

Ultimate Road Runner displays ads through Appodeal, a third-party ad mediation platform. Appodeal and its advertising partners may collect the following information from your device to serve relevant ads:

  • Advertising ID — a resettable, non-permanent identifier assigned by your device (Android Advertising ID). You can reset or opt out of this in your device settings under Google → Ads.
  • IP address — used to determine your approximate region for ad targeting and fraud prevention.
  • Device information — device model, OS version, screen size, and language, used to serve correctly formatted ads.
  • Ad interaction data — whether you viewed or tapped an ad, used to measure ad performance.

This data is collected and processed by Appodeal and their network partners, not by us. We do not receive, store, or have access to any of this information.

Appodeal Privacy Policy

Appodeal's full privacy policy, including the list of all advertising network partners and their data practices, is available at:

https://appodeal.com/privacy-policy/

We strongly encourage you to review Appodeal's privacy policy to understand what data their partners may collect and how it is used.

GDPR — Users in the European Union and UK

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). When you launch the game in a GDPR region, you will be presented with a consent dialog managed by the Appodeal SDK. You may choose to consent to personalised advertising or decline, and personalised ads will be enabled or disabled accordingly.

You may withdraw consent at any time by resetting your Advertising ID in your device settings (Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID) or by opting out of ads personalisation entirely.

CCPA — Users in California, USA

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected about you and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. Because the game itself collects no data, these rights apply specifically to Appodeal and its partners. Please refer to Appodeal's privacy policy linked above.

Children's Privacy

Ultimate Road Runner is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Data Security

Since we collect no data ourselves, there is nothing for us to secure or store. Data collected by Appodeal is handled under their own security practices, which you can review in their privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the game after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
