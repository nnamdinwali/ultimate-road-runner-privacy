1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Road Runner (the “Game”) handles information when you use the application. The Game is a mobile arcade game developed by Nnamdi Nwali. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

The Game itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not operate user accounts, login systems, or any servers that receive data from players. Your gameplay, scores, and progress remain solely on your device.

3. Third-Party Advertising

The Game displays advertisements served by Yandex Mobile Ads, a third-party advertising platform operated by Yandex LLC. Yandex and its advertising partners may collect the following information from your device for the purpose of serving and measuring advertisements:

  • Advertising Identifier (Android Advertising ID) — a resettable device identifier used for ad targeting and frequency capping. You can reset or opt out of ad personalisation at any time in your device Settings under Google → Ads.
  • IP Address — used to determine approximate geographic location for ad delivery and fraud prevention.
  • Device Information — such as device model, operating system version, screen resolution, and language settings, used to serve properly formatted advertisements.
  • Ad Interaction Data — records of whether an advertisement was viewed or clicked, used solely for measuring advertising performance.

This data is collected and controlled by Yandex. Nnamdi Nwali does not receive, access, or store any of this information.

4. GDPR — European Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) gives you certain rights regarding your personal data. You may withdraw consent to personalised advertising at any time by resetting your Android Advertising ID in your device settings or by opting out of ads personalisation entirely under Google → Ads in your device settings.

5. CCPA — California Users

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) gives you the right to know what personal information is collected about you and to opt out of the sale of your personal information. Because Nnamdi Nwali does not collect any personal data from the Game, these rights apply specifically to Yandex and its advertising partners. Please review Yandex’s privacy policy for details.

6. Children’s Privacy

Road Runner is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you have reason to believe that a child under 13 has provided personal information through the Game, please contact us at jacksonmich972@gmail.com and we will take appropriate action.

7. Data Security

As Nnamdi Nwali collects no personal data, there is no data for us to secure or store on our end. Data collected by Yandex is subject to Yandex’s own security practices and policies.
